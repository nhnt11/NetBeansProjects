The 1st Layer
The Cross

The first step to solving the cube is to make a cross or '+' (plus) on top of the cube. This sounds easy, but the catch is that all four edges have to match the center cubes below them. Take a look at the applet below for an example:

Remember that when putting in the edges, it doesn't matter if the colors are not oriented with their respective centers at first - you can always adjust the U layer later. What matters is getting them in the right orientation with the other 3 edges (ex. Blue opposite Green)
The Corners
